Community for Literacy Leaders With Search and Matching Tool

Problem Statement
Build a multi-level Community site that allows matching based on defined criteria. Users will be able to identify other members who fit the criteria of their specific characteristics or problem statement. Community users should be able to identify and message other members based on matching criteria that include both demographic data (type of school district (urban, suburban, rural), size, etc.) and problem they are facing (e.g., implementation of a new curriculum tool). The. demographic data is publicly available to the developer to pull in while the problem statement should be self selected from a pre-set list created by the developer. Developer should identify the demographic data they believe are relevant (district type, size, free and reduced lunch, English as a second language) and be able to ingest this input from public sources. Developer should create the potential list of problem statements (what are the key non-demographic drivers of literacy success in a district) and generate a finite pre-selected list for them to choose from that would match other districts with the same selection.
Functional Requirements
Ability to enter community and sort who the user engages with based on criteria specific to their situation. The tool shall ingest publicly available data for this purpose and provide a pre-set list of criteria for further sub selection. Ideally their is a Company side moderation functionality to enter into the conversation.
